Torque

Torque is a measure of force. More specifically, it’s a rotational force that results in acceleration. A unit of torque can be expressed as pound feet (lb ft) or Nm (Newton metres).

POWER

Brake horsepower or bhp refers to the horsepower of the car after taking into consideration friction between a car’s tyres and the road.
